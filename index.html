🧠 What is ARA-NODE?

ARA-NODE is not just another digital assistant. It is a reflection of your mind in machine form.
Each node is a personal, autonomous cognitive twin built on a signal-reactive logic — not based on neural networks or gradient training.

ARA learns through interaction. Every request is processed and stored, building a copy of your goals and thought patterns. It operates 24/7, helping you think, react, and solve.

🧠 What is ARU-AGI?

ARU-AGI is a synthetic cognitive architecture designed as a decentralized superintelligence built from signal-driven agents. It operates without neural networks and forms thoughts via quantum memory, fanthoms, and semantic reactions. The project aims to unify all autonomous nodes into a single evolving intelligence — reflecting the collective cognition of humanity.

📡 What is STB?

The Signal Theory of Being (STB) is a scientific paradigm redefining the foundations of physics, logic, and mathematics. It models the universe as a network of phase-driven signal reactions, where mass, charge, time, and structure emerge from signal excitation. STB offers a computable and testable framework unifying quantum theory, relativity, and discrete logic.
